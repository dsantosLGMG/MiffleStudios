Studio Structure

Miffle Studios manages multi-business logistics under an advanced, unified administrative layout encompassing nine operational office hubs.

Governing center holding ultimate executive and board level development authority across administrative policy and studio initiatives.

Office 02

Office of Production

Managing standard delivery blocks, milestones, pipeline timelines, asset maps, and tracking inter-departmental delivery tasks.

Office 03

Office of Writing & Narrative

Architecting multi-ending branches, deep lore, character backstories, rich worldbuilding frameworks, and heavy dialogue scenarios.

Office 04

Office of Public Relations (PR)

Overseeing external branding, corporate positioning releases, media alignment networks, and official studio messaging channels.

Office 05

Office 06

Tracking allocation charts, equipment assets procurement logs, hardware spending analysis reports, and administrative studio capital tracking.
